Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TravelNest itinerary planning tools.

How is the Destination of the Day selected? ▼
The Spotlight Destination changes automatically every 24 hours at midnight. It utilizes a deterministic calendar date hashing algorithm, cycling through our global registry database so that every user sees the exact same highlighted destination daily.
Is my travel budget data safe? ▼
Yes! All your travel budgets, saved wishlists, explored status settings, and newsletter preferences are stored safely and locally inside your browser's localStorage database. No financial information is sent to external servers, protecting your privacy.
How does the Random Trip Generator choose alternative matches? ▼
If our database lacks an exact match for your selected vibe and budget level, the system triggers a fallback queue. It first searches for other destinations matching your travel vibe (across other budgets), then tries to match your budget (across other vibes), and finally falls back to recommending a top destination from our full list.
Can I use TravelNest offline as a PWA? ▼
Absolutely! TravelNest is fully structured as a Progressive Web Application (PWA). It registers a background Service Worker that caches stylesheets, images, HTML documents, and scripts, allowing you to open and run the planner dashboard even without internet connections.
